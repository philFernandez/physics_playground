by Phil Fernandez.
  • The boxed off area to the left over here is the playground
  • In the far left margin we have the controls
  • Double clicking anywhere in the playground will give birth to a new shape
  • By default that will be a circle. You can switch to rectangles in the controls
  • New shapes will be static until you press the play button, at which time there after all shapes will be subject to physics, until you press pause
  • As long as physics are active you can pick up any object with your mouse and interact with it. Throw it around, knock it into other objects, etc.
  • This is not optimized for mobile (yet)
